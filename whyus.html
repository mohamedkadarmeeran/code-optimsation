Why Us?

All our recruitment specialists come from UX background, we do not employ a non-UX'er. We easily understand your needs technically and are in a better position to evaluate and assess a person's skillsets and ultimately place right candidates for the right job. Our specialists regularly self-educate on new technologies.

How are we different?

We are a group of highly enthusiastic UX/UI developers and designers with over 15-20 years of experience who know the industry inside out.

USP (Why Choose Us?)

Well trained and uniquely capable staffing consultants guided by UX experts with over 15+ years of experience. The candidates we place well adjust to your work schedule and timings.

problem statement

Hiring and finding a person concentrated at doing one thing presents a challenge. With ever-changing programs and latest improvements in software, UX took a wider and Bigger leap into the world.

Our Solution

We have a very well trained and carefully handpicked list of candidates and we do the selection process through various in-depth screening, helping us in finding and getting the best and providing you with the employee you been searching.
